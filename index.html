Frequently Asked Questions

What is StatVerdict?

StatVerdict is an AI-powered loot analysis platform built for Action RPG players. Upload a screenshot of any in-game item and get an instant, detailed evaluation ‚Äî including a score, build-specific recommendations, and whether you should keep, salvage, or equip the item. Our first supported game is Diablo IV through our Horadric AI scanner, with more ARPGs coming soon.

How does it work?

You upload a screenshot of an item tooltip from your game. Our AI reads the image using optical character recognition (OCR), identifies every stat, affix, and property on the item, then evaluates it against your selected class and build. The result is a letter grade (S through D), a verdict (Keep, Salvage, Equip, Sanctify), and a detailed breakdown of why.

Is it free to use?

Yes! StatVerdict is free to use. You provide your own Gemini API key (Google's AI service), which has a generous free tier. This means your scans are processed directly through your own API key ‚Äî we never see your data, and there's no subscription or paywall on our end.

Why was StatVerdict created?

As ARPG players ourselves, we got tired of alt-tabbing to wikis, asking Discord, or guessing whether a drop was actually good. We wanted a tool that could look at an item and instantly tell you if it's worth keeping ‚Äî tailored to your specific class and build. StatVerdict was built to solve that problem, starting with Diablo IV and expanding to every major ARPG.

Which games are supported?

Currently, Diablo IV is fully supported through our Horadric AI scanner, including all 7 classes, Sanctified item detection, Greater Affix analysis, and the Season 11 Heavenly Forge system. Path of Exile 2, Last Epoch, and additional Diablo titles (D3, D2R, Immortal) are on our roadmap ‚Äî you can vote for the next game on the landing page.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your API key is stored locally in your browser only. Item screenshots are sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API for analysis ‚Äî they never pass through our servers. Scan history, loadout data, and preferences are all stored locally in your browser. We don't collect personal information or track individual users.

What is Horadric AI?

Horadric AI is the Diablo IV scanner within StatVerdict. Named after Diablo's iconic Horadric Cube, it's the engine that analyzes your D4 items. It supports Analyze mode (single-item evaluation), Compare mode (side-by-side item comparison), a persistent loadout system, scan journal, and integration with Diablo.Trade for market lookups.

How do I get a Gemini API key?

Visit Google AI Studio and sign in with your Google account. Click "Create API Key" ‚Äî it's free and takes about 30 seconds. Copy the key and paste it into the Settings panel in Horadric AI. The free tier gives you plenty of scans per day for normal use.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes! You can screenshot an item on your console or PC, then scan it from your phone. The interface is optimized for touch with large tap targets and mobile-friendly layouts.

How can I contribute or report issues?

We'd love your feedback! Use the "Report Issue" or "Suggest a Game" links in the footer to submit feedback via Google Forms. You can also follow us on Instagram @StatVerdict for updates, previews, and community polls. Vote for the next supported game in the voting section above!
