Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Diablo 4 AI loot analyzer work?

StatVerdict acts as an AI gear checker for Diablo 4 and other ARPGs. Simply upload a screenshot of an item tooltip from your game. Our engine reads the image, identifies every stat and property, and evaluates it against current Diablo 4 item stat weights for your selected class. The result is an instant keep or salvage recommendation tailored to your specific build.

Is this D4 item scanner safe to use?

Yes, absolutely. Because our tool operates entirely in your browser through image uploads, it does not inject into game memory or violate any Terms of Service. Your data is private: images are processed in real-time and never saved on our servers, making it the safest way to quickly sort inventory in D4.

How do I know if I found a God Roll or if I should salvage it?

Stop guessing "is this item an upgrade in Diablo 4?" Our D4 god roll checker analyzes base stats, Aspect rolls, and what to do with Greater Affixes. It grades the item (S through D) and gives you a definitive "Keep", "Salvage", or "Equip" verdict based on whether the stats align with top meta builds.

How do I compare items in D4 to my equipped gear?

Our Diablo 4 loot analyzer includes a dedicated "Compare" mode. You can upload a screenshot of your current gear alongside a new drop to see a side-by-side breakdown. The AI will explicitly tell you which item yields better performance, taking the guesswork out of min-maxing your loadout.

Will this work for Path of Exile 2 or Last Epoch?

Currently, our primary scanner (Horadric AI) is optimized as a Diablo 4 item scanner. However, full support for Path of Exile 2, Last Epoch, and other ARPGs is actively in development. You can vote for the next game to be added in the voting section above!

Is it free to use?

Yes! StatVerdict is 100% free to use. There are no subscription fees, no paywalls, and no API keys required. You get full access to our instant visual intelligence and loot filtering tools right out of the box.
