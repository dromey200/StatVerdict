## Features Explained

### 📸 Gear Scanner

**What it does:** Analyzes your gear screenshots and tells you if you should keep or salvage the item.

**How to use:**
1. In Diablo IV, hover over any item to show the tooltip
2. Take a screenshot:
   - **Windows:** Press `Print Screen` or use Snipping Tool
   - **Console:** Use your platform's screenshot feature
   - **Mobile:** Take a photo of your screen
3. Upload to StatVerdict (drag & drop or click to browse)
4. Fill in the context fields
5. Click "Analyze Item"

**Pro Tips:**
- Make sure the tooltip is fully visible and readable
- Screenshots should be clear (not blurry)
- Accepted formats: PNG, JPEG, WebP (max 10MB)

---

### 🎯 Class Selection

**What it does:** Tells the AI which class you're playing so it can evaluate stats correctly.

**Why it matters:**
- A stat that's amazing for Barbarians might be useless for Sorcerers
- Class-specific mechanics change what stats are valuable
- Some items are universally good, others are class-specific

**Example:**
- **Barbarian:** Strength, Fury generation, Vulnerable Damage
- **Sorcerer:** Intelligence, Mana, Cooldown Reduction, Burning Damage

**Options:**
- Any Class (generic analysis)
- Barbarian
- Druid
- Necromancer
- Rogue
- Sorcerer
- Spiritborn

---

### 📊 Character Level (NEW!)

**What it does:** Adapts the AI's recommendations to your actual progression stage.

**Why it matters:**
A Legendary sword that's "mediocre for endgame" might be **INCREDIBLE for Level 25**. Without your level, the AI doesn't know if you're:
- Still leveling through the campaign
- Just hit 60 and starting Torment
- Paragon 50 optimizing your first build
- Paragon 200 pushing Pit 150

**How to enter your level:**

| Your Situation | What to Type | Example |
|----------------|--------------|---------|
| Leveling (1-60) | Just the number | `25` or `58` |
| Paragon player | "Paragon" + number | `Paragon 120` |
| Paragon player (short) | "P" + number | `P85` or `p200` |

**What you'll get:**

**Level 25:**
> "This is an INCREDIBLE find for your level! This weapon will carry you through the entire campaign and beyond. Keep this equipped until you reach max level."

**Paragon 120:**
> "Best-in-Slot weapon for your Paragon tier. This is worth full masterworking investment. The high rolls make this perfect for pushing higher Pit tiers."

**No level entered:**
> "KEEP - This is an endgame Best-in-Slot weapon perfect for high-tier Pit pushing. Worth full masterworking investment."

### ⚙️ Advanced Settings

**What it does:** Fine-tune the analysis for your specific build.

**When to use:**
- You have a specific build focus (e.g., Critical Strike DoT Rogue)
- You want ultra-precise recommendations
- You're optimizing for specific content (Pit pushing vs Speed farming)

**Options:**

#### 1. Build Mechanics
- **General:** No specific mechanics focus
- **Critical Strike:** Crit chance/damage focused builds
- **Damage Over Time:** Bleed, Poison, Burning builds
- **Summoner:** Minion-based builds (Necro, Druid)
- **Tank/Defensive:** Survivability-focused builds
- **Movement/Speed:** Speed farming, mobility builds
- **Resource Generation:** Resource-hungry builds

#### 2. Build Focus
- **Balanced:** General purpose
- **Max Damage:** Pure DPS optimization
- **Survivability:** Defensive stats priority
- **Speed Farming:** Clear speed + movement
- **Pit Pushing:** High-tier endgame content
- **PvP:** Player vs Player builds

**Example:**
If you're playing a **Rogue** at **Paragon 80** using a **Critical Strike** build focused on **Pit Pushing**, the AI will:
- Prioritize Crit Chance/Damage stats
- Recommend items that scale into high-tier content
- Suggest masterworking strategies for your Paragon level

### 🏆 Grading System

Every item gets a **letter grade** that tells you its quality at a glance:

#### S Tier - Best-in-Slot 👑
**Badge:** Crown icon, Purple/Pink gradient  
**Verdict:** BiS (Best-in-Slot)

**What it means:**
- Perfect or near-perfect rolls for meta builds
- Top-tier stats and synergies
- Definitely keep this item

**For Endgame Players:**
Lock this item immediately and prioritize masterworking. This is BiS gear.

**For Leveling Players:**
Exceptional find for your level - use this until max level. You found endgame gear early!

---

#### A Tier - Excellent ✨
**Badge:** Sparkles icon, Green gradient  
**Verdict:** KEEP & USE

**What it means:**
- Strong rolls with high build synergy
- Ready for endgame content with minimal investment
- Great find, definitely keep

**For Endgame Players:**
Ready for high-tier content - worth masterworking to optimize.

**For Leveling Players:**
Very strong for your level - keep this equipped as you progress.

---

#### B Tier - Solid ⭐
**Badge:** Star icon, Blue gradient  
**Verdict:** UPGRADE POTENTIAL

**What it means:**
- Good foundation with room to improve
- Usable for progression
- Consider upgrading at the Enchantress

**For Endgame Players:**
Has potential - consider enchanting or low-tier masterworking.

**For Leveling Players:**
Good stats - suitable for campaign progression.

---

#### C Tier - Below Average ⚠️
**Badge:** Alert Triangle icon, Yellow/Orange gradient  
**Verdict:** REPLACE SOON

**What it means:**
- Mediocre stats or poor synergy
- Too many wasted affixes
- Use if you have nothing better, but replace ASAP

**For Endgame Players:**
Too many wasted affixes - replace as soon as you find something better.

**For Leveling Players:**
Usable if you have nothing better - replace when you find an upgrade.

---

#### D Tier - Poor 🗑️
**Badge:** Trash icon, Red gradient  
**Verdict:** SALVAGE NOW

**What it means:**
- Wrong stats for your build
- Very low rolls
- No value - salvage immediately

**For Endgame Players:**
No value - salvage immediately for crafting materials.

**For Leveling Players:**
Wrong stats or terrible rolls - salvage and keep looking.

## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to enter my level?
**A:** No, it's optional! But entering your level makes the analysis **way more accurate** because the AI understands your actual progression.

**Without level:** "This item is mediocre for endgame" *(might salvage a great leveling item)*  
**With level (25):** "This is INCREDIBLE for Level 25! Keep this equipped" *(correct advice)*

### Q: What if I make a mistake selecting my class?
**A:** Just click "New Scan" and upload the image again with the correct class. Your history will still have the old scan if you want to compare.

### Q: Can I compare two items side-by-side?
**A:** Not yet! This is a planned feature for a future update. For now, scan both items separately and compare the grades/analysis.

### Q: How accurate is the AI?
**A:** The AI is trained on current meta knowledge and stat priorities. However:
- Seasonal changes may shift what's "meta"
- Personal playstyle matters (not everything needs to be "optimal")
- Use the analysis as a guide, not gospel

### Q: Does this work for all item types?
**A:** Yes! Weapons, armor, accessories, gems - anything with a tooltip works.

### Q: What about Ancestral/Sacred items?
**A:** The AI considers item power level in its analysis. Higher item power = higher potential even with similar stats.

### Q: Can I use this on console?
**A:** Yes! Take a screenshot on your console, transfer it to your phone/computer, then upload it to StatVerdict.

### Q: Is my data private?
**A:** Absolutely. All analysis happens in your browser, and scan history is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store your screenshots.

## Progression Tiers Explained

The AI categorizes players into **6 progression tiers** based on your level input:

| Tier | Level Range | Focus |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Early Leveling** | 1-29 | Campaign progress, basic stats |
| **Late Leveling** | 30-59 | Transitioning to endgame-ready items |
| **Fresh 60** | 60 (no Paragon) | Entering Torment, building first endgame set |
| **Early Paragon** | Paragon 1-49 | Early endgame, resource-conscious masterworking |
| **Mid Paragon** | Paragon 50-149 | Build optimization, Pit progression |
| **Endgame** | Paragon 150+ | Min-maxing, high-tier Pit pushing |

**Why this matters:**
An S-tier item means different things at different tiers:

**S-Tier at Level 20:**
> "This will carry you through the entire campaign and beyond. Incredibly rare find for your level."

**S-Tier at Paragon 150:**
> "Perfect rolls for endgame meta. Lock and fully masterwork immediately. BiS for Pit 150+."

## Troubleshooting

### "My image won't upload"
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Verify file format (PNG, JPEG, WebP only)
- Try a different browser
- Compress the image if it's too large

### "Analysis is taking forever"
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Reload the page and try again
- Try a smaller image file
- If it consistently fails, report the issue

### "The grade seems wrong"
**Remember:**
- Grades are relative to current meta
- Your build focus affects recommendations
- Leveling vs endgame context matters
- Personal playstyle may differ from meta

If you think there's a genuine error, consider:
- Did you select the correct class?
- Did you enter your level accurately?
- Are you using a non-meta build that the AI doesn't account for?

### "My history disappeared"
**Common causes:**
- Browser data cleared
- Incognito/private mode (history doesn't persist)
- Different device/browser
- localStorage disabled in browser settings

**Prevention:**
- Export important scans (take screenshots of results)
- Use the same browser/device
- Don't clear browser data if you want to keep history

## Glossary

**BiS (Best-in-Slot):** The absolute best item for a specific slot in an optimized build.

**Paragon:** Post-level-60 progression system. Players continue gaining Paragon levels (P1, P2, P3...) after hitting max level.

**Pit:** Endgame activity (The Pit of the Artificers) used for high-tier progression and masterworking materials.

**Torment:** Difficulty tier system in endgame (Torment I, II, III, IV).

**Masterworking:** Endgame upgrade system that enhances item stats at the Blacksmith using Pit materials.

**Greater Affix:** Enhanced version of a stat that appears at higher item power levels.

**Item Power:** Numeric rating (600-925) that determines stat ranges. Higher = better potential rolls.

**Meta:** Most effective tactics available - the currently optimal builds/items according to community/theorycrafters.
