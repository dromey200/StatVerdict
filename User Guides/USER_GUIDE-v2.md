# Horadric AI — User Guide

**Your AI-Powered Diablo IV Gear Analyst**  
**Version:** 3.0.0  
**For:** Players of all levels — Campaign to Paragon 300+

## Welcome

Horadric AI (powered by Stat Verdict) is your intelligent companion for evaluating Diablo IV gear. Whether you're Level 15 figuring out if that new sword is worth equipping, or Paragon 200 optimizing for Pit 150, we provide context-aware analysis tailored to your specific situation.

**What makes it different:**
- Level-aware analysis — tell us your level, get advice that actually applies to you
- No endgame bias — equally valuable for leveling and endgame players
- Build-specific — factor in your class, build style, and key mechanics
- Auto-detect — upload any screenshot and the AI figures out if it's a single item or a comparison
- Instant results — upload, analyze, decide in seconds
- No setup required — no API keys, no accounts, no subscriptions

## Quick Start (30 Seconds)

1. Visit the Scanner (you're already there when you land on the site)
2. Upload a screenshot of your gear tooltip
3. Select your class (e.g., Barbarian, Sorcerer, Paladin)
4. Optionally enter your level (e.g., "45" or "Paragon 120")
5. Click "Analyze Item"
6. Get your verdict — keep it or salvage it

That's it. No account needed, no subscription, no ads.

## Features

### Gear Scanner

The Scanner is the main page and your primary tool. Upload any Diablo IV item screenshot and get an instant AI analysis.

**How to take a screenshot:**
- Windows: Press Print Screen or use the Snipping Tool
- Console: Use your platform's screenshot feature (Xbox: Xbox button + Y, PlayStation: Share button)
- Mobile: Take a photo of your screen with your phone camera

**Accepted formats:** PNG, JPEG, WebP (max 10MB)

**Upload methods:**
- Drag and drop your screenshot directly into the upload zone
- Click the upload zone to browse and select a file

**Auto-detect:** You don't need to tell the AI whether your screenshot shows one item or two items side-by-side. Upload any screenshot — the AI automatically detects if it's a single item tooltip or a comparison view and responds accordingly.

### Class Selection

Selecting your class is important because stats are valued differently per class. A stat that's amazing for Barbarians might be useless for Sorcerers.

**Available classes:** Any Class, Barbarian, Druid, Necromancer, Paladin, Rogue, Sorcerer, Spiritborn

When you select a specific class (not "Any Class"), the Advanced Settings section becomes available.

### Character Level

Tell the AI your character level for recommendations that match your progression. The AI categorizes you into one of six tiers:

| Tier | Level Range | What the AI focuses on |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| Early Leveling | 1-29 | Items that accelerate campaign progress |
| Late Leveling | 30-59 | Items that remain viable into endgame |
| Fresh 60 | 60 (no Paragon) | Transition advice for Torment tiers |
| Early Paragon | Paragon 1-49 | Resource-conscious masterworking recommendations |
| Mid Paragon | Paragon 50-149 | Build optimization and Pit progression |
| Endgame | Paragon 150+ | Min-max advice for high-tier content |

**How to enter your level:**

| Your situation | What to type |
|----------------|-------------|
| Leveling (1-60) | Just the number: `25`, `45`, `60` |
| Paragon player | "Paragon" + number: `Paragon 120` |
| Paragon (short) | "P" + number: `P85`, `p200` |

If you leave it blank, the AI defaults to endgame-level advice.

### Advanced Settings

When you select a specific class, you can expand Advanced Settings for more precise analysis:

**Build Style** — Your specific build archetype. These are class-specific:
- Barbarian: Whirlwind, Hammer of the Ancients, Upheaval, Thorns, etc.
- Druid: Werewolf Tornado, Pulverize, Stormclaw, Landslide, etc.
- Necromancer: Bone Spear, Minion Army, Blood Surge, Infinimist, etc.
- Paladin: Juggernaut (Shield Tank), Zealot (Melee DPS), Judicator, Disciple
- Rogue: Twisting Blades, Penetrating Shot, Rapid Fire, Barrage, etc.
- Sorcerer: Ice Shards, Firewall, Ball Lightning, Meteor, etc.
- Spiritborn: Jaguar Rush, Eagle Evade, Centipede Poison, Gorilla Tank, etc.

**Key Mechanic** — The core mechanic your build relies on. Also class-specific:
- Barbarian: Berserking Uptime, Bleed Damage, Fury Generation, etc.
- Rogue: Critical Strike, Vulnerable Damage, Combo Points, etc.
- Sorcerer: Mana Generation, Cooldown Reduction, Crackling Energy, etc.

**Build Focus** — Your gameplay priority (same for all classes):
- Balanced: General purpose
- Max Damage: Pure DPS optimization
- Survivability: Defensive stats priority
- Speed Farming: Clear speed and movement
- Pit Pushing: High-tier endgame content
- PvP: Player vs Player builds

### Grading System

Every item gets a letter grade that tells you its quality at a glance:

**S — Best-in-Slot** (Crown icon, purple/pink)
Perfect or near-perfect rolls for meta builds. Keep permanently. Lock and masterwork immediately.
- Endgame: BiS gear — lock immediately
- Leveling: Exceptional find — use until max level

**A — Excellent** (Sparkles icon, green)
Strong rolls with high build synergy. Ready for endgame content with minimal investment.
- Endgame: Ready for high-tier content — worth masterworking
- Leveling: Very strong — keep equipped as you progress

**B — Solid** (Star icon, blue)
Good foundation with room to improve. Usable for progression, worth upgrading at the Enchantress.
- Endgame: Has potential — consider enchanting or low-tier masterworking
- Leveling: Good stats — suitable for campaign progression

**C — Below Average** (AlertTriangle icon, yellow/orange)
Some useful stats but too many wasted affixes. Replace when something better drops.
- Endgame: Too many wasted affixes — replace ASAP
- Leveling: Usable if nothing better — replace when possible

**D — Salvage** (Trash icon, red)
Wrong stats, poor rolls, or no value for your build. Salvage immediately for materials.
- Endgame: No value — salvage immediately
- Leveling: Wrong stats — salvage and keep looking

### Scan History

All your scans are automatically saved (if auto-save is enabled in Settings). You can:
- Search items by name
- Filter by rarity (Mythic, Unique, Legendary, Rare, Magic)
- Filter by grade (S through D)
- Click any item to view the full analysis again
- Delete individual items or clear all history

History stores the last 20 scans. Images are not saved to prevent storage issues.

### Settings

Click the gear icon in the header to access Settings:

**Auto-save scan results** — Toggle on/off. When enabled (default), every analysis is automatically saved to your History. When disabled, you can scan without cluttering your history.

**About** — Version info, season info, links to report issues or submit enhancements.

### Help Tour

Click the question mark icon in the header to start a guided tour of the interface. The tour highlights each section (Scanner, Tutorial, Rating Guide, History, Settings) with a brief explanation.

## Tips for Best Results

### Screenshot Quality
- Make sure the tooltip is fully visible and readable
- Crop to just the item tooltip if possible — smaller images process faster
- Avoid blurry or pixelated screenshots
- Avoid screenshots with overlapping UI elements blocking stats

### Context Accuracy
- Select your actual class — don't use "Any Class" if you know your class
- Enter your real level — it dramatically improves recommendation accuracy
- Use Advanced Settings when you have a specific build — generic analysis is less precise

### Comparison Screenshots
- The AI auto-detects side-by-side comparisons — just upload the screenshot as-is
- Make sure both tooltips are fully visible
- The AI typically evaluates the NEW item (right side) against the EQUIPPED item (left side)

### Using History Effectively
- Scan multiple items, then compare grades in History
- Track your progression over time — see if your average gear grade improves
- Use the detail view to review past analysis reasoning

## Troubleshooting

### "Your image is too large to process"
- Crop your screenshot to just the item tooltip instead of full screen
- Ensure the file is under 10MB
- Try saving as JPEG instead of PNG (smaller file size)

### "Something Went Wrong" with no specific message
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few seconds — the AI service may be temporarily busy
- Try a different screenshot

### "Too many requests"
- The AI service has rate limits — wait a moment and try again
- Avoid rapidly submitting multiple scans

### "This appears to be from an unsupported game"
- Only Diablo IV is currently supported
- Make sure your screenshot shows a D4 item tooltip, not D3, D2R, or Diablo Immortal

### History disappeared
- History is stored in your browser's local storage
- Clearing browser data removes history
- Incognito/private mode doesn't persist history
- History is per-device and per-browser

### Auto-save toggle doesn't seem to work
- Open Settings (gear icon in header)
- The toggle is red when enabled, gray when disabled
- Changes take effect on the next scan — existing history is not affected

## Glossary

**BiS (Best-in-Slot):** The absolute best item for a specific equipment slot in an optimized build.

**Paragon:** Post-level-60 progression system. Players gain Paragon levels (P1, P2, P3...) after hitting max level.

**Pit:** The Pit of the Artificers — endgame activity used for high-tier progression and masterworking materials.

**Torment:** Endgame difficulty tiers (Torment I through IV).

**Masterworking:** Endgame upgrade system that enhances item stats at the Blacksmith using Pit materials.

**Greater Affix:** Gold-bordered stat with 1.5x value. More powerful than normal affixes.

**Sanctified:** Items blessed at the Heavenly Forge. Permanent optimization — cannot be further modified after sanctification.

**Item Power:** Numeric rating (700-925) that determines stat ranges. Higher means better potential rolls.

**Meta:** Most effective tactics available — the currently optimal builds and items according to the community.
