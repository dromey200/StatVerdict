Every item is evaluated against your class, build, and current meta. Grades reflect real endgame value — not just item power.

Grade Verdict What It Means
S Best-in-Slot Perfect or near-perfect rolls for meta builds. Keep permanently — this is endgame gear.
A Excellent Strong rolls with high build synergy. Ready for Pit pushing and endgame content.
B Solid Good foundation with room to grow. Usable for progression; worth upgrading at the Enchantress.
C Below Average Some useful stats but too many wasted affixes. Replace when something better drops.
D Salvage Poor synergy, low rolls, or wrong stats for your build. Salvage for materials.

Special Modifiers

🦋 Sanctified Heavenly Forge candidate. Evaluated for permanent upgrade potential.
✨ Greater Affixes Gold-bordered stats with 1.5× value. Each one significantly boosts the grade.
💜 Mythic Ultra-rare unique. Almost always S-tier — the AI evaluates build fit, not just rarity.

Leveling items (Common, Magic, Rare) are graded on a separate curve based on level-appropriate usefulness, not endgame standards.
